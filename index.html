About Us

Mitra Barito Group

Mitra Barito Group is a diversified business conglomerate based in Barito Utara, Central Kalimantan specializing in mining, contracting, heavy equipment rental, logistics, healthcare, port services, and property development. With years of experience and a strong network of industry partners, we provide comprehensive, integrated solutions tailored to the needs of contractors, mining companies, industrial projects, and urban development across Indonesia.

Mitra Barito Rental

Mitra Barito Rental is a professional provider of heavy equipment rental and mining support services, committed to delivering high-quality solutions with a strong focus on safety, reliability, and timely operations

We offer a wide range of rental durations, skilled operators, and a professional equipment delivery system.

Backed by a dedicated technical team and a well-maintained fleet, Mitra Barito Rental is ready to be your trusted partner in mining operations.

Mitra Barito Contractor

Mitra Barito Contractor is a trusted partner in mining and infrastructure development, specializing in contracting services for excavation, hauling, and land development projects. We are committed to delivering high-performance results through efficient project execution, professional manpower, and reliable equipment.

With years of experience in the industry, Mitra Barito Contractor supports clients with a comprehensive solution, from planning and operations to post-project evaluation, ensuring quality, safety, and environmental compliance.

Mitra Barito Logistics

Mitra Barito Logistics provides efficient, reliable, and safe logistics solutions to support mining, industrial, and infrastructure operations. Our services are designed to optimize the flow of goods, materials, and equipment—ensuring timely delivery and operational continuity.

With a dedicated fleet and experienced team, we handle complex logistics challenges across remote and demanding environments.

Mitra Barito Healthcare

Mitra Barito Healthcare – Cardiac Clinic is committed to delivering comprehensive heart care with precision, compassion, and advanced medical technology. Our clinic specializes in preventive cardiology, diagnosis, and treatment of various heart conditions.

With a team of experienced cardiologists and modern diagnostic tools, we ensure early detection, accurate assessment, and effective management of cardiovascular diseases.

Mitra Barito Port Services

Mitra Barito Port Services provides specialized logistics and handling solutions for coal port operations, ensuring efficiency, safety, and reliability in every process.

Strategically located to support regional mining activities, our port services are equipped to manage high-volume coal transfers with modern infrastructure and professional operational standards.

Mitra Barito International Trading

Mitra Barito International Trading is a subsidiary of Mitra Barito Group, focusing on the trading and export of high-quality coal products. We supply a variety of coal grades to meet the diverse needs of our clients in the energy and industrial sectors.

  • - Coal GAR 4200
  • - Coal GAR 5200
  • - Coal GAR 6500

We focus on building strong partnerships, navigating complex regulations, and delivering exceptional service. Our portfolio includes a wide range of products, from raw materials to finished goods, all sourced from trusted suppliers.

PT. KPN

Contractor & Hauling

We, Mitra Barito Group (MBG), serve as the main contractor for KPN, providing end-to-end mining and hauling services. Our role includes site development, overburden removal, coal extraction, and transportation, ensuring efficient and safe operations in line with industry standards.

PT. GBM

Contractor & Hauling

PT. Global Bara Mandiri operates as a coal hauling and logistics provider with an annual capacity of 1 million tons.

PT. RIYANTA JAYA

Contractor & Hauling

PT. RJ has been operating as a contractor and hauling service provider from 2022 to 2024, supporting mining operations in Central Kalimantan. With a focus on reliability and efficiency, PT. RJ contributed to key coal transportation and site development projects during this period.

PT. Kapuas Bara Utama

Hauling

Kapuas Bara Utama Hauling is a dedicated coal transportation operation with a hauling capacity of 2 million tons per year. Strategically located to support mining activities in Central Kalimantan, it ensures efficient, reliable, and continuous coal delivery from pit to port, contributing to the smooth logistics chain of the region’s coal industry.

PT. TELEN ORBIT PRIMA

Hauling

We serve as the contractor for PT. TOP, with a production target of 30,000 tons. Our responsibilities include managing site operations and ensuring efficient coal extraction and delivery, in line with safety and performance standards.
